About Me

Former shareholder value creator turned desert ground sloth. Reestablishing a relationship with time, place, and people. Reskilling in the Sonoran desert, harvesting rainwater to grow community foodscapes, collecting solar energy to light our way. Sharing the abundance. Building dual power.Or whatever the non-hierarchical version of dual power is... multi-power? manifold power?

Attempting to keep the keyboard at more than arms length on average, but when it's within reach, I'm often making music, art, code, or some combination of all three.

Exploring lately: optimistic visions of the future (social ecology, degrowth, usufruct, permaculture), food sovereignty, natural building, from-scratch cooking, crafts pushed aside in the descent to rampant consumerism, and figuring out how to carefully and minimually use technology in all of the above.

I sometimes share things on Mastodon.
